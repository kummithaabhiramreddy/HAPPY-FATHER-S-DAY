✦ From My Soul to Yours ✦

My Wishes
for You, Dad

If I could give you the whole world wrapped in gold, I would — but these wishes come from somewhere even deeper.

🎂
☀️

Joy Every Morning

May every sunrise bring you a reason to smile. May your mornings be peaceful, your evenings restful, and your days filled with small beautiful moments that remind you how loved you are.

🏥

Perfect Health

I wish you a body as strong as your spirit, a heart that beats with vigour for many more decades. May illness stay far from your door and may you wake every day feeling alive and well.

🕊️

Deep Peace

You have worked and worried your whole life for us. I wish you peace — the kind that settles in your bones, that makes you sit back and say "all is well." You deserve rest, Dad.

🌈

Endless Blessings

May God pour blessings on you that you cannot even count. Blessings in your home, blessings in your name, blessings that follow you everywhere you go and multiply with every step.

💰

Abundance & Prosperity

You gave us everything without hesitation. Now I wish that abundance returns to you multiplied — that every need is met, every dream funded, and your wallet never tells you "no."

💞

A Love-Filled Home

May our home — your home — always be warm, full of laughter and love. May the people around you bring you pride and joy. May you never feel alone, not even for a single moment.

🌟 Happy Father's Day, Dad! 🌟

Today the world celebrates fathers — but in our home, we celebrate you every single day. You are not just a great father, you are a great human being. We are so unbelievably lucky to call you ours.

Blessings I Pray for You

Every night before I sleep, I ask for these things for you.

🙏
ProtectionMay you always be safe, guarded by angels, free from danger and harm in every step you take.
💛
HappinessMay a deep, unshakeable happiness root itself in your heart — not dependent on circumstances but born from within.
🌿

⭐
